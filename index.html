Research Projects

LDVAE-T

A novel deep learning model for hyperspectral unmixing that combines transformer architectures with a Latent Dirichlet Variational Autoencoder. Designed to capture spatial and spectral context, the model estimates material abundances in hyperspectral imagery with significantly improved accuracy. My work included model design, implementation, and extensive evaluation on benchmark datasets.
